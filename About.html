Welcome to my online portfolio where I showcase my interests in 3D Modeling, photography, and music. I hope you enjoy looking around! Feel free to contact me through LinkedIn if you’d like to get in touch.

I was introduced to 3D modeling when I was ten years old. I started by creating a copy of my elementary school in Google Sketchup and posting it on Google Earth. Since then, I’ve become a self-taught modeler in Blender. Now I model for online competitions, for 3D printing projects, and for fun. I’m interested in learning more and having a career in computer-aided design.

I've been doing photography and photo editing for eight years. In that time I've mainly focused on landscapes, and I especially enjoy taking pictures on vacation in Arches, Zion, the Redwoods, and other national parks. I started on a handheld digital camera, and now I use a Nikon DSLR. I also enjoy the photo editing process, and I'm an avid Adobe Photoshop user.

I’ve been playing trumpet since 5th grade. I have taken private lessons from Bob Peterson and am currently a student of David Blackinton. I am a member of the Timpview Symphonic Band (1st chair), Jazz Band (2nd chair), and Marching Band (section leader). I was also a part of the Utah All State Band for 2016, and I have received a superior rating at State Solo Ensemble in 2015 and 2016. I especially enjoy the teamwork and leadership opportunities that come from playing in an excellent high school band. During my time at Timpview, we have received numerous marching band awards, including 1st in division and sweepstakes.
